There have been some significant developments in Turkey‚Äôs relations with both Israel and UAE in the post-2013 period. After the Mavi Marmara or otherwise known as Gaza Flotilla incident (2010) where Israeli security forces stormed a boat carrying humanitarian aid to Gaza killing ten activists (mostly Turkish citizens), Turkish-Israeli relations broke. Turkey‚Äôs increased engagement in the Middle East and its support for Islamist parties introduced significant tensions between Turkey and the Gulf countries like UAE and Saudi Arabia.
